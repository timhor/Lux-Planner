Help & FAQs

What benefits me from using LUX?

By using LUX, you can freely plan out all your journeys and exactly what you intend to do at each location before making any commitments to booking flights, accommodation, etc.

Do I need to pay any fees to access the website?

No, LUX is free to use for everyone.

How do I sign up?

Creating an account allows you to access additional features within the website, such as saving the journeys you create.

  1. Click Sign Up at top of any page.
  2. Enter a unique username, password of at least 8 characters, email, first and last name.
  3. Click on the CREATE ACCOUNT button. You will then be automatically logged in.
How do I log in?
  1. Click Log in at the top of any page.
  2. Enter your username and password.
  3. Click on the LOG IN button.
How do I create a journey?

Creating a journey is the primary function of this website. You may create a 'trial' journey to test the functionality of the site; however, if you are not logged in, you cannot save any journeys you create.

  1. If you're on the home page, enter your starting location in the search bar and click on the New Journey button. Otherwise, click New Journey at the top of any page.
  2. Enter a name for the journey, starting location, next location, and your respective arrival and departure dates
  3. If you want to add additional stops: click ADD STOP and repeat step 2 for the new stop.
  4. Save your newly created journey.
    • If you are logged in: click on the SAVE JOURNEY button. The journey will then be stored under your account.
    • If you are not logged in: click either the SIGN UP or LOG IN buttons to proceed.
